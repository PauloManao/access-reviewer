About

  • Mission

    To legitimize people with mobility difficulties by providing a web application that allows them to easily access information about accessibility, encouraging them to move independently and participate in social life.

  • Vision

    We envision to become a model mobility-friendly platform that helps the Society to build a world with no barriers for everyone.

  • Values

    We are commited to allowing people to have access information regardless their mobility limitations and this is intrinsic in all of our values: Inclusivity, Empowerment, Accessibility, Innovation and Responsability.
